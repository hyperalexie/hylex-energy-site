Project Intelligence
[·]ScopePlex

Early signal on the industrial and energy projects that matter.

ScopePlex is Hylex's software capability — a project-intelligence tool built for business-development teams at EPC, MEP, and specialty construction contractors. It monitors public records to surface real, early-stage projects before they hit the trade press.

How It Works

Public records in, verified project signal out.

ScopePlex is built around primary-source monitoring — the kind of data that's structurally hard to copy because it isn't republished trade-press content.

1

Sources

Permit filings, utility interconnection queues, regulatory dockets, and public project records — monitored continuously.

2

Extraction

AI-driven extraction identifies and classifies real project milestones from raw source documents.

3

Verification

Extracted records are checked and scored before being surfaced — signal, not noise.

4

Delivery

Verified project records reach BD teams through a digest and dashboard, built for daily use.

Built For

Business development teams who need signal earlier.

ScopePlex is designed for BD directors and teams at specialty, EPC, and MEP contractors who currently rely on trade press, generic alerts, or expensive enterprise project databases — and want an earlier, more direct read on what's actually being built.
