About Hylex

One accountable partner, across the full project lifecycle.

Hylex Energy Consulting is an integrated industrial project solution provider. Energy and industrial projects fail on coordination, not intent — so we built Hylex around five capabilities that cover a project end to end, delivered through one relationship.

Why Five Capabilities

A single point of accountability.

Operators, EPCs, and service companies typically coordinate fabrication yards, valve and pump vendors, engineering firms, downhole tool suppliers, and internal BD tracking tools separately — with no single party accountable for the whole picture. Hylex exists to remove that coordination burden.

Global Presence

Driving projects from concept to commissioning.

Strategic fabrication and engineering hubs across North America and Asia mean Hylex delivers seamless project execution — anywhere your project demands.

Houston, TX — Global Support Hub

The strategic center for engineering excellence and program governance

  • Front-End Project Development: early engagement, feasibility studies, and cost modelling to de-risk decisions before fabrication.
  • Engineering & Design Coordination: multi-discipline oversight, 3D modeling integration, and technical alignment with EPC requirements.
  • Procurement Intelligence & Global Sourcing: vendor prequalification, material strategy, and commercial negotiation.
  • Client Interface & Program Management: single point of contact for tracking, milestone reporting, and change management.
  • QA, Inspection & Audit Support: U.S.-based QA specialists and NDE coordination, including third-party audits.
China — Fabrication & Engineering Hub

The backbone of our global delivery platform

  • World-class fabrication capacity and heavy engineering expertise
  • One of the largest heavy industrial fabrication and assembly yards in Asia
  • Full-scope pipe spooling, pressure vessels, structural steel, and complete process skid systems delivered under ISO, ASME, and PED standards
  • Mature domestic supply-chain ecosystem with redundancy across suppliers
Vietnam — Fabrication & Engineering Hub

Scale, flexibility, and speed

  • Specializing in heavy structural fabrication, modular assembly, and integrated process units
  • Vast yard capacity and deep-water load-out infrastructure
  • Tariff-resilient alternative to China-based supply chains for NA, EU, and Australia-bound projects
Monterrey, Mexico — Fabrication & Engineering Hub

A cornerstone of our North American delivery platform

  • World-class fabrication capacity with proximity to North American markets
  • Schedule-driven, cost-effective execution with seamless logistics
  • USMCA trade advantage — reduced tariffs and simplified customs
  • Highly skilled, licensed, and accredited local workforce
