Oilfield Solutions

Downhole and water-management technology for oilfield operations.

Two specialized technology tracks purpose-built for demanding well and process conditions — proprietary water treatment and separation systems, and dissolvable downhole tools engineered for full-bore, no-mill completions.

Water Treatment & Separation

Advanced process design, proprietary solutions.

Proprietary process systems and separation packages that dramatically improve efficiency, reduce OPEX, and solve production bottlenecks — proven across 10+ offshore and onshore deployments.

High-Efficiency Dehydration System

Removes 85%+ water from production streams with <50% water cut. Reduces oil-in-water (OIW) concentration to <30 mg/L. Compact footprint, zero moving parts, ultra-low operating costs.

Hydrocyclone & Compact Flotation Units (CFU)

Hydrocyclone reduces OIW from <1000 mg/L to <100 mg/L. Compact flotation further reduces OIW to <30 mg/L. Proven across 10+ offshore projects.

Gas-Water Ultrafiltration Membrane System

Removes particulates and organic matter 0.002–0.1 μm. Delivers 10× higher anti-fouling performance than conventional membranes. Achieves Class A1 water quality for reinjection.

CFIC Biofilm Wastewater Treatment

Up to 99% media fill rate, eliminating clogging issues. Significantly reduces footprint and energy consumption. Ideal for high-COD, high-turbidity wastewater in offshore and petrochemical applications.

Dissolvable Downhole Products

Full-bore completions, no drilling or milling.

Dissolvable magnesium alloy tools engineered for automatic dissolution after fracturing — full-bore wellbore, reduced job risk, and lower comprehensive cost versus traditional drillable plugs.

Soluble Magnesium Alloy Materials

23 standardized material grades engineering tensile strength, dissolution rate, and temperature tolerance to well conditions — backed by 10+ related material patents.

Soluble Bridge Plugs

Single-slip O-ring, single-slip all-metal, single-slip packer-element, and double-slip packer-element variants — customized to well conditions, sealing duration, and dissolution time required.

Soluble Rubber

Temperature-graded elastomer series (25°C to 150°C+) for packer elements, seals, and downhole temporary sealing parts — no-residue, milling-free dissolution.
