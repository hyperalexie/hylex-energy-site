Full-bore completions, no drilling or milling.

The Products

Soluble by design.

Soluble Magnesium Alloy Materials

23 standardized material grades engineering tensile strength, dissolution rate, and temperature tolerance to well conditions — backed by 10+ related material patents, including the world's earliest patent for soluble magnesium alloy materials.

Soluble Bridge Plugs

Single-slip O-ring, single-slip all-metal, single-slip packer-element, and double-slip packer-element variants — customized to well conditions, sealing duration, and dissolution time required.

Soluble Rubber

Temperature-graded elastomer series (25°C to 150°C+) for packer elements, seals, and downhole temporary sealing parts — no-residue, milling-free dissolution.

Testing & Verification

Third-party tested, well-condition proven.

Dissolvable bridge plug products undergo third-party testing at CNAS/CMA-accredited organizations, meeting the SY/T 7462-2019 standard. LS-DYNA dynamic simulation verifies the sealing process — stress-strain state of slips, cones, and rubber cylinders at any time, confirming embedding depth and anchoring reliability before field deployment.
