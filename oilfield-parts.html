Oilfield Solutions · Parts

Full-bore completions, no drilling or milling.

Dissolvable magnesium alloy downhole parts engineered for automatic dissolution after fracturing — full-bore wellbore, reduced job risk, and lower comprehensive cost versus traditional drillable plugs.

The Products

Soluble by design.

Single-Slip O-Ring

Low rubber proportion, superior temperature adaptability across low-temperature dissolution and high-temperature pressure resistance.

Single-Slip All-Metal

Operating temperature range from -20°C up to 210°C.

Single-Slip Packer Element

Wide sealing surface for adaptability to slight casing deformation under low and medium temperatures.

Double-Slip Packer Element

Bidirectional anchoring and pressure resistance, with a wider sealing surface for superior sealing performance.

Soluble Magnesium Alloy Materials

Soluble Rubber

Testing & Verification

Third-party tested, well-condition proven.

Dissolvable bridge plug products undergo third-party testing at CNAS/CMA-accredited organizations, meeting the SY/T 7462-2019 standard. LS-DYNA dynamic simulation verifies the sealing process — stress-strain state of slips, cones, and rubber cylinders at any time, confirming embedding depth and anchoring reliability before field deployment.
