At the heart of every successful project is engineering that transforms complex requirements into buildable, optimized, and cost-effective solutions — from front-end studies through detailed design, fully integrated with procurement and fabrication.

Core Capabilities

Full-spectrum engineering, one team.

Our multidisciplinary approach ensures seamless integration from front-end studies to detailed design, supporting every phase of the asset lifecycle.

Front-End Engineering & Design (FEED)

Feasibility studies, conceptual layouts, and early cost modelling to de-risk project decisions. Process flow diagrams (PFD), P&IDs, and basis of design documents aligned with client objectives.

3D Modeling & Digital Twin Development

Full 3D plant modeling with clash detection, routing optimization, and digital twin integration. Smart modeling platforms ensuring constructability and real-time design coordination.

Detailed Design & Shop Drawings

Piping isometrics, structural fabrication drawings, equipment layouts, and installation schematics. Fully build-ready documentation integrated with procurement and fabrication workflows.

Process, Mechanical & Structural Engineering

Pressure vessel and heat exchanger design to ASME/EN/PED standards. Modularization, skid layout engineering, and structural calculations optimized for transport and installation.

As-Built & Lifecycle Engineering

Red-line integration, as-built documentation, and predictive maintenance data generation. Continuous engineering support for modifications, upgrades, and expansion projects.
